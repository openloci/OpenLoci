## What This Is

**{{ cookiecutter.palace_name }}** is an OpenLoci palace wearing the **Clue** skin — the base chassis, unmodified. The Tudor mansion. The nine rooms. The six suspects. One of them did it.

{{ cookiecutter.project_description }}

The DM is the LLM. The player is the investigator. The mystery may or may not have a solution.
