## What This Is

**{{ cookiecutter.palace_name }}** is an OpenLoci palace wearing the **Muppets** skin over the **Clue** chassis. The Tudor mansion becomes the Muppet Theater. The suspects become the cast. The mystery is whatever happens when you put this many personalities in one building and ask them to put on a show.

{{ cookiecutter.project_description }}

The DM is the LLM. The player is the investigator. It ain't easy being green.
